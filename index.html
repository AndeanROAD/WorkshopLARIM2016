Rationale

The creation of the Andean Regional Office of Astronomy for Development (ROAD) was formalized in October 2016. The Andean ROAD gathers 20+ institutions in Bolivia, Chile, Colombia, Ecuador, Peru and Venezuela.

In this workshop at LARIM2016 we want to:

  • Present the Office of Astronomy for Development (OAD) and the Andean ROAD.
  • Map the principles and projects that will guide the OAD and the Andean ROAD in coming years.
  • Explore possibilities in which the LARIM community can join forces with the Andean ROAD.
